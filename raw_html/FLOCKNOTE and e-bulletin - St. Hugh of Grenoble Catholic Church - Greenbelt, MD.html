FLOCKNOTE and e-bulletin

  • How we communicate
    The pandemic has changed so many things. As a parish, we realize how important it is that we communicate with each other quickly and pastorally.
    From our weekly e-bulletin, to our daily links for our livestream Masses, or texts to quickly remind or update, FLOCKNOTE is our means. 
    FLOCKNOTE is a secure and private system that allows us to email and text our parishioners and friends.
